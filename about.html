The Prime Prophet

Prophet Edward Agyeben, widely known as the Prime Prophet (PP), is an international prophet with a global mandate to reveal the power, presence, and purpose of God in the lives of believers across the nations.

From a young age, the hand of God was evident upon his life with remarkable prophetic manifestations and a deep passion for prayer. After years of spiritual preparation and mentorship, he stepped into full-time ministry, carrying a burden to see lives transformed through the prophetic.

Today, Prophet Edward ministers internationally through physical tours and online platforms, reaching hundreds of thousands with the message of hope, healing, and spiritual awakening.
